Examination Papers 1995-96

This is still an experimental service. All 1995-96 exam papers set within the Department of Computing (with the exception of ISE-only papers) have been scanned in and made available as pdf files. You will need Adobe Acrobat Reader to read them. This is installed on all the UNIX teaching machines. It is not currently available on either the macs or PCs.

Note only three of the five laser printers in the Teaching Labs are capable of printing pdf files. These are the printers in rooms 203, 208 and 219. The printers in rooms 206 and 207 do not support Level 2 PostScript, and will only print an error sheet.

Thanks to Frank Kriwaczek for his help and advice, to Keith Sephton for installing Acrobat Reader, and to Maureen Munday (and Mehran Zonouzi) for doing the scanning.

Papers 1.1-1.9 and 2.1-2.12 will be available on paper from the SGO, priced £ 1.50. The 1994 and 1995 papers have not been scanned, and will continue to be available from the SGO.

You may find that the quality when viewed on screen is not very good, though it should be adequate if you increase the magnification. Nevertheless the print quality should be fine.

You may wonder why the papers are scanned in and made available as gifs. This is because the printed papers are produced by a variety of means, and so this is the only systematic way to guarantee a complete set of papers which are exactly as set.
