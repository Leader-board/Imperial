Department of Computing Examinations 1997-98

Examination Papers 1998

All 1997-98 exam papers set within the Department of Computing (with the exception of ISE-only papers) have been scanned in and made available as pdf files. You will need Adobe Acrobat Reader 3.0 to read them. This is installed on all the Linux and Windows NT systems in the teaching labs.

We used a new scanning system this year. Thanks are due to: Frank Kriwaczek for his help and advice, to Chris Hogger for creating the Web pages, to Mark Dawson, Niranjan Jayasundera, Christos Stergiou and Robert Chatley from CSG for purchasing and configuring the equipment, to Teresa Sergot, Barbara Claxton and Andy Davies.

You may find that the quality when viewed on screen is not very good, though it should be adequate if you increase the magnification. Nevertheless the print quality should be fine.

You may wonder why the papers are scanned in and made available as gifs. This is because the printed papers are produced by a variety of means, and so this is the only systematic way to guarantee a complete set of papers which are exactly as set.

Iain Phillips
December 1998
